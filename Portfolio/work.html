What kind of is a good leader?​

A good leader should have these characteristics:

Self-Awareness: He has an intimate knowledge of his inner emotional state. He knows his strengths and his weaknesses. He knows when he’s working in flow and he knows when he’s over worked.

Self-Direction: He’s able to direct himself effectively and powerfully. He knows how to get things done, how to organize tasks and how to avoid procrastination.

Ability to motivate: Leaders don’t lead by telling people what they have to do. Instead, leaders cause people to want to help them.

Vision: He’s working towards a goal that’s greater than himself. It could be something small, like the success of the team, or a larger vision like world peace.

Social Awareness: Understanding social networks and key influencers in that social network is another key part of leadership. Who in the organization has the most clout, both officially and unofficially? Who moves the hearts of the group?

Polar Bear Pitching

About event.

Polar Bear Pitching is an event for startups to present their ideas to investors. They must be present while they are immersed in an ice pack to undergo pressure from the cold. Time is unlimited, but cold tolerance is the only limit.

What is the best in the event?

This is a good opportunity for startups to present their ideas not only to investors but also to promote their products to customers.

Investors have the chance to view multiple presentations and ideas from multiple startups and examine its competitiveness.

Some examples of the business ideas

Spark Horizon: They produce the power supplier for every electric cars which targets a clean energy source and protects the environment.

Kelver: This is an application that allow user get all bills and pay for only one time.

Business Kitchen

About.

Business Kitchen is an active group worked by OAMK and Oulu University.

This is an open coworking space at OUAS Kotkantie campus and at Oulu University. They receive funds from EU Grant and BusinessOulu also.

Their target is changing the way of learning for students who want implements their business ideas under the Business Kitchen help and promote. Beside that, they give the local companies resources of university to improve their business.

What did I learn?

Avanto idea accelerator is pre-startup that accelerate the transform of ideas into business on workshop, one-on-one coaching, mentoring and networking.

International Business Corridor (IBC) is that international student teams help companies to expand abroad. They will cooperate with their local companies and partner universities.

Demola Oulu is that student teams create solutions (demos) for real-life challenges that provided by companies and innovate solutions in key role.

Terwa-academy specialize to entrepreneurship with first year basic business studies.

Oulues (Oulu entrepreneurship society) promote entrepreneurship among students through events, competitions, workshop and networking.

Geogang

This is an application that can diagnose a disease and other skin problem through take the picture and analyze it on skin or some other body parts.

Eatwhere

This is a restaurant finding application that allow users search based on: food types, location, price and rate of a restaurant, then it will display its location and navigate you to there on map.

Their target market are customers in Oulu can use smartphone and cooperation restaurant. They make delivery by themselves and offer it to restaurants.

Ticketr

They are a sale ticket system that will sale events ticket with the lower prices than they are. They remove ticket touting and resell to get the profit as much as possible.

Their target market are nightlife and event organizers, free events, large scale festivals. For free event, they cannot earn profit from this, but they get 2.0% for each ticket sale for two target markets remain.

Mobitaxi

This is an application for a particular taxi company. Basically, this application works similar to UBER. They allow users enter 2 points on a map and contact to the nearest driver of taxi company.

The different point is that they build a platform and sell it for companies but not manage taxi system. Their fee for use their platform is 1000e per year, and companies can create as well as modify the service.

WatchTube

This is a streaming platform for gaming. It offers the system that allow streamers create and manage their channel. There is no limit about viewer to access one channel and follow it.

Their target is streamer on all of age and sponsor want to advertise on streamer channel.
