What kind of is a good leader?​

A good leader should have these characteristics:

Self-Awareness: He has an intimate knowledge of his inner emotional state. He knows his strengths and his weaknesses. He knows when he’s working in flow and he knows when he’s over worked.

Self-Direction: He’s able to direct himself effectively and powerfully. He knows how to get things done, how to organize tasks and how to avoid procrastination.

Ability to motivate: Leaders don’t lead by telling people what they have to do. Instead, leaders cause people to want to help them.

Vision: He’s working towards a goal that’s greater than himself. It could be something small, like the success of the team, or a larger vision like world peace.

Social Awareness: Understanding social networks and key influencers in that social network is another key part of leadership. Who in the organization has the most clout, both officially and unofficially? Who moves the hearts of the group?
